ResolveIQ

An AI-powered IT support agent that resolves issues in chat — or files the ticket for you.

55% of issues resolved at first contact through self-service — no ticket required.

The Challenge

Why this mattered

Internal IT support at a leading enterprise SaaS and cloud infrastructure provider was stretched thin. A complex service portal and manual ticketing kept employees waiting and support staff buried in routine work.

😣

Portal fatigue

Employees avoided the complex service portal, flooding the helpdesk with unstructured emails and Teams messages instead.

⏱️

Slow ticket creation

Support staff spent 40% of their time manually logging, categorising, and assigning tickets rather than resolving them.

📚

Knowledge base underused

Over 2,000 KB articles sat unused — employees raised tickets even for simple password resets and VPN fixes.

🎯

Inconsistent prioritisation

Priority was assigned subjectively, so critical issues sat in the queue behind routine requests.

🌙

No after-hours support

Support ran 9-to-6, leaving employees in other time zones with no self-service option for common issues.

The Solution

What we built

ResolveIQ is a fully low-code Copilot Studio agent — the first point of contact for every internal IT request. Employees describe their issue in plain language in Teams; ResolveIQ either resolves it instantly from the knowledge base or creates a prioritised ticket and routes it to the right team automatically. No portal hopping, no manual logging, no queues.

💬

Natural-language intake

Employees just describe the problem in Teams chat — no forms, no portal, no ticket fields to fill in.

⚡

Instant self-service

Pulls answers from the ServiceNow knowledge base to resolve common issues on the spot, without a ticket.

🎫

Automatic ticketing

When a ticket is needed, ResolveIQ creates it, sets priority, and assigns the right support team — automatically.

📊

Rule-based prioritisation

Consistent priority and SLA-based escalation replace subjective, case-by-case judgment.

🌍

Always on

24/7 self-service means employees in any time zone get help the moment they need it.

🛠️

Owned by IT ops

Fully low-code — the IT operations team maintains and extends it with no developer dependency.

How It Works

Solution architecture

A single conversation in Teams flows through the agent to either an instant answer or a prioritised, auto-routed ticket — with everything logged for analytics.

3

4

→
5

Confirm & log

Adaptive Card in Teams; Dataverse records history and analytics.

  1. Capture — An employee types their problem into the ResolveIQ chat in Microsoft Teams.
  2. Interpret — The Copilot Studio agent reads the request and determines whether it can be self-served.
  3. Branch — For known issues, ResolveIQ returns a ServiceNow KB article instantly; otherwise it raises a ticket.
  4. Orchestrate — Power Automate applies routing logic, sets priority by rule, and assigns the correct support team, with SLA-based escalation.
  5. Confirm & record — An Adaptive Card confirms the outcome in Teams while Dataverse logs the conversation, ticket metadata, and analytics.
